Sembène!

Director

Samba Gadjigo

Jason Silverman

Release Year

2015

Runtime

"Sembène!" is a documentary celebrating Ousmane Sembène, the father of African cinema, known for groundbreaking films like "Borom Sarret" and "Black Girl." The film explores his life and work, championing African cinema's role in speaking to African audiences.
